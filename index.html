About MS

Welcome to MSHospital

Due to high speed of increase of populations of Mogadishu, Somalia which estimated around 4.9 million people and need of high quality health services are needed in the Mogadishu and neighboring regions lower shabele and middle shabelle regions responded to their demand,

Mogadishu Specialist Hospital was the first multi-disciplinary hospital in Mogadishu healthcare which was established 01/08/1997 in Mogadishu, Somalia by health professionals. As a healthcare facility, it has continued to dominate Mogadishu's healthcare landscape in terms of the quality of its medical staff, the breadth of services it offers and the standard of equipment it operates
